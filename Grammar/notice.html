Notice Writing

What is notice writing ?

Notice writing is a formal method of communication used to inform people about important events, meetings, announcements, or warnings. It’s often seen on notice boards in offices, schools, housing societies, etc. Writing a notice follows specific rules and formats to ensure clarity and efficiency.

Format of Notice Writing

Notice writing is the easiest writing piece that students can go for. Just like letters and articles, notice writing too has a particular format. In fact, marks are often allotted for the format itself during evaluation. So if you want to get full marks in notice writing, you have to learn how to design and present a notice that would fulfil its purpose.


Basic Elements of a Notice:



  • Name of the Organisation: The first thing to write is the name of the organisation/school/college from where the notice is being issued.

  • Title: The title for any notice writing is the word “Notice” itself.

  • Date: The next important thing that students must keep in mind while drafting the notice is to write the date.

  • Heading: The heading of the notice refers to the subject of the notice.

  • Body: The body contains the main content of the notice.

  • Signature and Designation: The notice ends with the signature of the person who composes the notice and their designation.

Tips for Notice Writing


  • No details: Notice writing is not about details but about the exact information you need the readers to know.

  • Correct Heading: Since the notice is meant for a large audience, it’s important that they understand what the notice is about.

  • Format: It is very crucial that the writer maintains the format of the notice.

  • Clarity: Even if notices are meant to be short, you have to make it clear to the readers what the notice wants to convey.


Notice Writing Template

Name of the School/College/Organisation

NOTICE

Date

Title of the Notice

Body of the Notice

Signature

DESIGNATION


Sample Notice - 1

National Model Public School, Chennai

NOTICE

14/01/2022

Parent-Teacher Conference

The Parent-Teacher Conference will be held on the 20th of January, 2022. It is mandatory for all parents of Std 10 students to attend the conference. The conference will go on for two hours (10 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.) All parents will have to assemble in the Main Auditorium by 9:45 a.m.

Signature of the Principal
